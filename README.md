#Financial Ratio Analysis - TechGrow Ltd

Overview
Analyzed 5 years of financial data for TechGrow Ltd to determine lending recommendation.

Skills Used
- Excel (formulas, calculations)
- Financial Analysis (ROE, ROA, Margins)
- Statistics (Mean, Standard Deviation)

 Key Findings
- Revenue growing 12% per year
- Net Margin stable at 20%
- ROE consistent at 20%
- Low standard deviation = low risk

 Recommendation
YES  Lend to TechGrow Ltd. Company is financially healthy with consistent growth and stable profitability.
